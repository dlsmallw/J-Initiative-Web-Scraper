The Home Page

This is the home page! This is the default page that opens up when you launch the program. In addition, the home page is where you can see any notifications you may have. We have given you some example notifications that you can play with. Click on "notifications" to see your notifications. Then you can click x to save your notifications but close the notifications box, or click dismiss to dismiss the selected notification. Click on the box itself to see or select different notifications.
